## 3. Operational Guide
### Summary of Key Operational Steps:
1. **Planning and Goal Setting**:
   - Define clear objectives and create a 3-6 month plan.
   - Choose a monetization strategy (e.g., live streaming, product promotion).
   - Select two benchmark accounts for learning and inspiration.

2. **Content Creation**:
   - Develop high-quality videos focusing on lighting, filters, and background.
   - Write compelling scripts using effective structures like PREP, pain-point solutions, step-by-step guides, and personal stories.
   - Practice delivery techniques including reading scripts, practicing gestures, and managing facial expressions.

3. **Technical Skills**:
   - Use mirrors to practice overcoming camera fear and improving natural expression.
   - Train eye movements, ensuring they are not static and convey emotion naturally.
   - Utilize hand exercises to improve diction and pronunciation clarity.

4. **Audience Interaction**:
   - Identify and understand your target audience’s needs and preferences.
   - Create content that resonates emotionally and provides value.
   - Engage with viewers through comments and interactive elements.

5. **Performance Analysis**:
   - Monitor key metrics like view duration and audience retention.
   - Analyze viewer feedback and adjust content strategy accordingly.
